About Me

Hi! I have completed my PhD at INSA Lyon within the LIRIS and LAGEPP research labs.

My research interests are at the edges of physics, control, and machine learning. I am deeply interested in physical simulation with deep learning, and looking forward to pursue my research in connected fields, such ML-based control, and robotics.

My background is in electrical engineering and signal processing (MSc), but I also own a degree in artificial intelligence (MSc). In parallel, teaching will always be a part of my career, as I am a "agrégé" teacher in engineering and computer science.
