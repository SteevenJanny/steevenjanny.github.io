Results

Frontier tracking: when queried on a streamline between areas of opposite vorticity, the interpolation module attends not only to the spatial neighborhood but also to the temporal flow near the frontier.

Blob tracking: the model tracks the origin of the perturbation, and focuses on its displacement. Our dynamic interpolation exploits the evolution of the state rather than merely averaging neighboring nodes.

Periodic boundaries: our model effectively leverages the periodic condition of the Navier dataset, especially when queried on points originating from perturbations on the other side of the simulation. Again, the interpolation depends on which points explain the output, rather than the neighborhood.
