MaGNet suffers from drastic shifts in distribution between training and evaluation. The model is trained on points from X , which corresponds to a small portion of the domain. We used our subsampling trick to artificially generate queries. During evaluation, we require the prediction at every available point in the complete simulation, hence, MaGNet must interpolate the initial condition to a large number of query points, filling the input of the auto-regressive model with noisy estimates of the IC.
